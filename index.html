About

Welcome to the Delicious Desserts website!! My name is Joanne and I created this website because of my love for desserts. Although I may not be a connoisseur of desserts, I very much appreciate these sweet tasting treats!! Ever since a young age, I've have always had a sweet tooth for some reason. It didn't help that I also tried to learn to bake from a young age as well. I remember starting off with the box mixes for brownies where all I had to add was an egg, water, and some oil. Even though it was simple, I had so much fun and waited with anticipation until my product was finished.

Eventually, I advanced from the box sets and precut cookies by trying out actual recipes from online. Trying out recipes was one of the best decisions I made because there can be so much more flavor and complexity from making something from scratch rather than doing the box mix version of it.

Although I'm not the best baker, I had my ups and downs from trying out different recipes of different desserts. I wanted to make this website in order to share my experience as a baker in order to inspire others to try it out!
